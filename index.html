    about racc

    Reading Area Community College (RACC)
    isa an accredited, comprehensive,
    open-enrrollment education institution
    that provides the following: Associate
    degree, Certificate and Diploma
    Programs; career-focused training;
    transitional coursework; skills training for
    business and industry; personal
    enrichment programs and public service
    activities. Sponsored by the Berks County
    Commissioners the College provides
    affordable access to meet identified
    community educational need.
